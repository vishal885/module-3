#Differentiate between append () and extend () methods?
->Append()=append object at end.
 Ex. x =[1,2,3]
     x.append([4,5])
     print(x)
     gives you:[1,2,3,[4,5]]
->Extend()=Extends list by appending elements from the iterable.
 Ex. x =[1,2,3]
     x.extend([4,5])
     print(x)
     gives you:[1,2,3,4,5]
